About Me

In 2018 I transitioned from the tourist and service industry towards web development. After deciding to take some time off work, I found myself diving back into web design, an old hobby of mine. I completed an intensive online Front-End Development TechDegree at Team Treehouse and continue to learn and experiment with coding on a daily basis. I have a strong understanding of HTML, CSS and JavaScript and various frameworks/libraries such as jQuery, Bootstrap and Materialize. I also enjoy building websites with Wordpress, and I am currently learning Vue.js.
